Chapter 5: Working Together

A week passed, and the turtle and the rabbit were still thinking about a way that each of them could set up a race that they could win. On Monday, the rabbit went to the turtle's house to challenge him in another race, but not a race in which one person wins, they both work together. The next day, they raced and...

The rabbit started off with the turtle, the rabbit took the turtle on his back and ran as fast as he could. Then after a few minutes, they arrived at the river, where the turtle took the rabbit on his lap and swam across the river. Again, when they went from the river to land, the rabbit once again took off with the turtle. They did this for a lot of times. They were close to finishing the race, and the turtle and the rabbit talked along the way, this was real collaboration, where both of them used their qualities to benifit themselves and the team. They finally finished the race, who won? Well, both of them did, because they worked together, without each other, none of them could finish the race.
